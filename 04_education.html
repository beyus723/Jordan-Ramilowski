Education


By teaching Practical Bioinformatics courses I help medical students and experimental researchers to learn basic theoretical and practical understanding of the functional Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) Data Analysis. Students mainly learn to write, debug and execute simple and reproducible R codes (using RStudio) to perform selected functional analysis (differential gene expression, pathway enrichment) and single-cell RNA-seq data analysis and to clearly present & report results of the analysis.
