AMRC (Advanced Medical Research Center/先端医科学研究センター) is located at Yokohama City University Medical Campus (Fukuura) and conducts basic studies aimed at clinical and medical applications. AMRC is involved in a number of R&D projects with Yokohama City University and external academic and industry researchers.

Bioinformatics Lab (link) is a part of AMRC and mainly conducts research in NGS analysis (see Research) and helps bioinformatics education of Master's / PhD Students and collaborative researchers (see Teaching).

We are always looking for motivated Japanese and International Students to join us long and short term! If you are interested, let us know in the contact form here.
